Who are we?

Agora is a digital platform designed to revolutionize the trading of grocery goods in New Zealand by creating an open and competitive marketplace. Through our innovative auction system, we empower buyers and sellers to negotiate and trade with confidence, free from legal restrictions or market actors. Our goal is to streamline the process for retailers by leveraging cutting-edge technology, helping them manage their trading strategy, increase opportunities, and foster a vibrant business environment.
