Agora is a digital platform that connects buyers and sellers in the New Zealand retail market. It provides a space where businesses can auction and sell goods directly to consumers or other businesses, using an efficient and transparent marketplace system.

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. You’ll need to provide your email address, set up a password, and choose whether you’re signing up as a buyer, seller, or business administrator.

On Agora, sellers list their products for auction, where buyers can place bids. The auction will run for a specified period, and the highest bidder at the end of the auction wins the item. The platform uses a transparent bidding system to ensure fair transactions.

Yes, Agora allows you to sell a wide range of products, including non-grocery items such as home goods, electronics, eco-friendly products, and more. Just ensure that your products comply with the platform's guidelines.

Payments on Agora are securely processed through our integrated payment gateway. You can use credit or debit cards to make payments. Sellers receive the payment once the transaction is complete, minus any applicable fees.

Agora charges a small transaction fee for each completed sale. The exact fee amount depends on the type of account and the product category. These fees are clearly outlined before confirming any sale.

Agora offers a messaging feature where buyers and sellers can communicate directly. You can either chat in groups or privately, depending on your preference, to discuss any details regarding the transaction.

Returns depend on the seller's return policy. When purchasing an item, please review the return and refund terms set by the seller. If there is an issue, you can reach out directly to the seller through the platform.

If you don't receive your item, you should first contact the seller through Agora's messaging system. If the issue isn’t resolved, you can file a dispute, and Agora will assist in investigating the matter.

Agora takes your privacy seriously. We employ industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information on how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

At this time, Agora primarily focuses on the New Zealand market. However, some sellers may choose to offer international shipping. Check the product listing or contact the seller directly to confirm shipping options.

To edit your account information, log in to your account and navigate to the "Account Settings" section. Here, you can update your personal details, password, and notification preferences.

Verified sellers have completed additional identity and business verification steps to provide an added level of trust for buyers. To become verified, apply through your account dashboard, and provide the required documentation.

Agora has a strict policy against the sale of illegal or restricted items, such as weapons, hazardous materials, counterfeit goods, and others. For a full list of prohibited items, please consult our Terms and Conditions.
